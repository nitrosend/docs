# Campaigns

A campaign is a one-time email send to a contact list or segment. Each campaign uses a template and tracks delivery metrics (opens, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes).

## Campaign vs Flow

- **Campaign** — manual, one-time send to a list. Good for newsletters, announcements, promotions.
- **Flow** — automated, event-triggered sequence. Good for welcome series, onboarding, re-engagement.

## Scheduling

Campaigns can be sent immediately or scheduled for a future date and time.
