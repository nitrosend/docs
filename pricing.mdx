# Pricing

Nitrosend is **pay per send, never per contact** — every plan includes unlimited
contacts. MCP, REST API, CLI, AI copywriting, campaigns, flows, transactional,
templates, and analytics are available on every plan, including Free.

<Info>
  Looking for full, programmatic pricing (enterprise volume tiers, overage
  rates, and add-ons)? Read the machine-readable pricing document at
  [nitrosend.com/pricing.md](https://nitrosend.com/pricing.md). It is the
  source of truth and is kept in sync with the [pricing page](https://nitrosend.com/pricing).
</Info>

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Emails / month | Brands | Seats |
|------|-------|----------------|--------|-------|
| **Free** | $0 | 8,000 then 500/mo | 1 | 1 |
| **Pro** | $20/mo | 20,000 | 1 | 3 |
| **Ultra** | $100/mo | 125,000 | 5 | 10 |
| **Enterprise** | from $300/mo | 500K–4M+ (tiered) | Unlimited | Unlimited |

- **Pro and above** add BYO sending/AI keys, frontier AI models, removable
  branding, and priority support.
- **Ultra and Enterprise** add dedicated IPs (a paid add-on, with automatic
  warmup) and more brands, seats, and domains.
- **Enterprise** adds SSO / SAML, a 99.9% SLA, a dedicated account manager, and
  tiered volume pricing up to 10M+ emails/month.

See the full feature comparison on the [pricing page](https://nitrosend.com/pricing).
