# Nitrosend

**The AI-native email marketing platform.** Built for builders and marketers who want to run their email marketing from the AI tools they already use.

Every ESP on the market was designed for humans to operate manually. You log in, click through menus, drag blocks, write copy, schedule sends. Nitrosend is different. It's built from the ground up so that AI does the work.

## How Nitrosend works

Instead of logging into a dashboard, you stay in your AI tool — Claude, ChatGPT, Codex, Gemini, Cursor — and tell it what you want. Nitrosend connects via **MCP (Model Context Protocol)** and a REST API so your AI assistant has full access to your email marketing.

```
You: "Create a 5-email welcome series for new subscribers"
Claude: Done. I've built the flow with a welcome email, 
        a 3-day follow-up, a product highlight on day 7, 
        a case study on day 10, and a special offer on day 14.
        Want me to send a test?
```

No dashboard. No drag-and-drop. Just tell AI what you need and it happens.

## What makes Nitrosend different

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-native, not AI-bolted-on">
    Other ESPs added GPT wrappers for subject lines and called it "AI features." Nitrosend was built so AI is the primary interface. MCP integration means Claude, GPT, and Gemini can access every feature — campaigns, flows, templates, contacts, analytics — natively.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="MCP-first architecture">
    Nitrosend speaks MCP (Model Context Protocol), the open standard for connecting AI tools to external services. Connect once, and your AI assistant can manage your entire email operation. No coding, no middleware, no webhooks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Intelligence that compounds">
    Set up your [Brand Settings](/integrations/mcp-brand-memory#brand-settings) and [AI Memory](/integrations/mcp-brand-memory#ai-memory), and every email Nitrosend sends gets smarter. Your AI learns your tone, your audience, and your goals — then applies that context to every campaign, flow, and template it creates.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Works with every major AI tool">
    Claude Desktop, Claude Code, Claude Cowork, ChatGPT, Cursor, Codex CLI, Gemini CLI, VS Code, Windsurf, Zed — Nitrosend connects anywhere the client supports MCP. Use the REST API for custom apps, automation platforms, and server-side integrations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Create your account at [app.nitrosend.com](https://app.nitrosend.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your AI tool">
    Add Nitrosend as an MCP server or connector — no API key needed for the standard setup. Follow the guide for your tool: [Claude Desktop](/integrations/claude-desktop) · [Claude Cowork](/integrations/claude-cowork) · [Claude Code](/integrations/claude-code) · [Cursor](/integrations/cursor) · [ChatGPT](/integrations/chatgpt) · [Codex](/integrations/codex) · [Gemini CLI](/integrations/gemini) · [VS Code](/integrations/vscode) · [Windsurf](/integrations/windsurf) · [Zed](/integrations/zed)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in when prompted">
    Most clients open a Nitrosend sign-in window on first use. If you are using a terminal client and the prompt does not appear immediately, open the client's MCP manager and finish OAuth there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start prompting">
    Try this to get a full setup in one go:

    ```
    Connect into Nitrosend and based on my brand, give me a suggested
    email flow to set up and guide me through set up. Make the email
    design modern and on brand against my website.
    ```

    Your AI will scrape your brand, suggest a flow, build it, and walk you through sending a test.
  </Step>
</Steps>
