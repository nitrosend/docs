# Claude Cowork + Nitrosend MCP

[Claude Cowork](https://claude.ai/cowork) is Anthropic's collaborative AI workspace. Connect Nitrosend via MCP and your team can manage email marketing through conversation — create campaigns, build flows, check analytics, and more, all within Cowork.

## What is MCP?

**MCP (Model Context Protocol)** is an open standard that lets Claude connect directly to external services. When Nitrosend is connected, Claude gets native access to 16 email marketing tools plus live resources about your brand, account, and email best practices.

## Setup: Add Nitrosend as a Connector

The connection method is the same as Claude Desktop — Cowork uses the same Custom Connector system.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your API key">
    Go to **Settings > API Keys** in the [Nitrosend dashboard](https://app.nitrosend.com) and copy your key (starts with `nskey_live_`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    In Claude Cowork, go to **Settings** > **Connections**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Nitrosend">
    Click **Add Custom Connector** and enter:
    - **URL**: `https://api.nitrosend.com/mcp`
    
    Click **Connect**, then authenticate with your API key when prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using Nitrosend">
    Nitrosend MCP is now available as a Connector in your Cowork workspace. Open a new conversation and start prompting:
    
    ```
    What's my Nitrosend account status?
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why Cowork for email marketing?

Cowork's collaborative workspace is particularly powerful for email marketing because:

- **Team context** — share conversations about campaigns, flows, and strategy with your team
- **Persistent projects** — keep email marketing as an ongoing project where Claude remembers your goals, brand, and past campaigns
- **Multi-step workflows** — complex tasks like "plan our Q2 email strategy, then build the flows" work naturally in Cowork's project format

## Example workflows in Cowork

### Plan and execute a campaign

```
Let's plan our March newsletter. Check my recent campaign performance, 
then create a new campaign targeting subscribers who opened the last 
email. Include a section about our new features.
```

### Audit your email setup

```
Review my Nitrosend account setup. Check my brand settings, sending 
domain health, and active flows. Are there any improvements you'd 
recommend?
```

### Build a complete flow

```
I need a complete abandoned cart recovery flow. Build a 3-email 
sequence: first email 1 hour after cart abandonment, second after 
24 hours, third after 3 days with a discount offer.
```

## Supercharge with Brand & Memory

For the best results in Cowork, make sure you've configured:

1. **[Brand Settings](https://app.nitrosend.com/my/settings/brand)** — your brand colors, fonts, logo, and company description
2. **[AI Memory](https://app.nitrosend.com/my/settings/memory)** — your marketing goals, target audience, and email strategy

Claude reads these automatically through MCP, so every email it creates matches your brand and follows your strategy.
